How long does it take to set up Fluxa?

+

Most teams can launch their first workflow in a few days. The timeline depends on your data sources, approval rules, integrations, and internal review process.

Can Fluxa connect with our existing tools?

+

Yes. Fluxa can connect with CRMs, internal databases, document systems, ticketing tools, and operational workflows through integrations or APIs.

Does the AI make final lending decisions?

+

Fluxa helps score, summarize, classify, and recommend next actions. Your team can keep approval gates, manual reviews, and final decisions where needed.

Can we customize our approval workflow?

+

Yes. You can customize rules, handoffs, approval stages, exceptions, permissions, and notifications based on how your lending team works.

Is Fluxa secure for financial data?

+

Fluxa is designed for secure operational workflows with role-based access, audit logging, approval controls, and enterprise-ready governance.

Can we start small and scale later?

+

Yes. Many teams start with one workflow, such as application intake or underwriting, then expand into payouts, repayment tracking, monitoring, and reporting.
